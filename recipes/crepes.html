Basic Crepes

Description:

This recipe is perfect for people who like healthy breakfasts. Crepes are very thin pancakes. They can be served with a wide variety of sweet and savory fillings and toppings.

ingreddients:

  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 tablespoons of butter, melted

Steps

    Step1:

    Whsk flour and eggs together in a large mixing bowl;gradually add in milk and water,
    stirring to combine. Add salt and melted butter; beat until smooth.

    Step2:

    Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop
    the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup each crepe. Tilt the
    pan with a circular motion so that the batter coats the surface evenly.

    Step3:

    Cook until the top of the crepe is no longer wet and bottom has turned light
    brown, 1 to 2 minutes. Run a spatula around the edge of the skillet to loosen
    the crepe; flip and cook until the other side has turned light brown, about 1 minute more. Serve hot.

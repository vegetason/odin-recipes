Honey Glazed Chicken

Description:

This recipe is great for the people who like to cook with few ingredients by following a few steps.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons of soya sauce
  • 2 skinless,boneless chicken breast halves,cut into bite-size pieces
  • 1/4 cup of honey
  • 1/8 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons of olive oil.

Steps:

  1. Step1:

    2. Whisk honey,soy sauce, and red pepper flakes in a boowl;set aside.

  2. Step2:

    3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat;cook and stir chicken in hot oil
    until lightly brown,about 5 minutes.

  3. Step3:

    4. Pour honey mixture into the skillet;continue to cook and stir until chicken is no
    longer pinkin the centerand sauce is thickened,about 5 minutes more.
